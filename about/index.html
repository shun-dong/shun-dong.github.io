Intro

采薇 是我自设的名字。我现在在读 数学 系本科，对各种理论都感兴趣，非常 open-minded，欢迎交流讨论。

我会发布一些 笔记，虽然有的可能略显粗糙，但都十分有趣。为了帮助理解，我做了一些很酷的 可视化。你也可以欣赏我的 作品。在此再次感谢 朋友们 的支持。

Kaelvio is the name I use for my original persona. I am currently an undergraduate student in Mathematics, interested in many kinds of theories, very open-minded, and always happy to communicate and discuss.

I publish some notes here. Some of them may still feel a little rough, but I find all of them genuinely interesting. To help with understanding, I have made some very cool visualizations. You can also take a look at my artworks. Once again, thank you to my friends for your support.

求知

Inquiry

我喜欢学习新知识和新技术，涉猎广泛（极端杂食）。

我对 代数 更感兴趣，现在的方向是 代数组合，同时对 理论计算机科学 有所了解，希望能从理论上发展 形式化数学。

写过一系列效率方面的程序，欢迎关注我的 GitHub 账号。

I enjoy learning new knowledge and new technologies, and my interests are very broad. I am especially interested in Algebra, and my current direction is Algebraic Combinatorics. I also have some understanding of Theoretical Computer Science, and hope to develop formalized mathematics from a theoretical perspective. I have written a series of productivity-related programs; feel free to follow my GitHub account.

人文

Humanities

我有很多人文社科和艺术方面的思考，会不时在 公众号 上写文章。我也自学过不少这方面的知识，主要关注的哲学流派有 存在主义、分析哲学、斯多葛主义。

我还热爱读书，正在读《我身上有个不可战胜的夏天》、On the Road、《金阁寺》。最近欣赏了 《挽救计划》、摇滚红与黑、《黑客帝国》、《禅与摩托车维修艺术》、《爱欲之死》、《撒哈拉的故事》、《悉达多》、《达摩流浪者》。

I have many thoughts on the humanities, social sciences, and arts. The philosophical traditions I mainly follow include Existentialism, Analytic Philosophy, and Stoicism. Recently, I enjoyed Project Hail Mary, Le rouge et le noir - L'Opera Rock, The Matrix, Zen and the Art of Motorcycle Maintenance, The Agony of Eros, Stories of the Sahara, Siddhartha, and The Dharma Bums.

行旅

Travel

我喜欢旅行和户外活动，经常去登山、徒步、骑自行车，也玩 飞盘（非常菜）……我现在每周都会出去转转，最近去了广州、伦敦、香港、澳门、深圳、重庆。

I enjoy traveling and outdoor activities. I often go mountaineering, hiking, cycling, and play frisbee (very much a beginner). I now go out almost every week, and have recently visited Guangzhou, London, Hong Kong, Macao, Shenzhen, and Chongqing.

游艺

Play

我喜欢小动物，（或许）是福瑞控，有一个 后启示录风格的世界观设定。平时 ACGN 都接触不深，并且大多是北极圈。我是 《猫武士》 冬青叶厨子，还比较喜欢 《来自深渊》、《罗小黑战记》、《大理寺日志》。

游戏的话我主要玩单机，时间随缘。我玩 《极乐迪斯科》、《光与影：33号远征队》、《塞尔达传说：王国之泪》、《丝之歌》（我真幸运）、《Undertale》、《Ori》……

I love small animals, and am perhaps a furry fan. I also have a post-apocalyptic worldbuilding setting. I am a big fan of Hollyleaf from Warriors, and I also like Made in Abyss, The Legend of Hei, and White Cat Legend. As for games, I mainly play single-player games whenever I have time: Disco Elysium, Clair Obscur: Expedition 33, Tears of the Kingdom, Hollow Knight: Silksong, Undertale, and Ori.
